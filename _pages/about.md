Hi there! My name is Joel. I'm a Catalan Ph.D. Candidate at the University of Toronto (Major in Comparative Politics, Minor in International Relations). I work on gender roles and intersectionality on political behaviour. Broadly, my interests also encompass the determinants, barriers and backlash against LGBTIQ+ rights in public policy, and the European Union public support.
